Leveraging machine learning and genomic data to unlock the potential of diverse rice landraces. This project studies unique genetic characteristics to enhance modern rice varieties for yield, stress tolerance, and nutrition.

Project Objectives & Methods

Our research combines advanced genomics with machine learning to:

  • Analyze genetic diversity in rice landraces using genomic data (in-house & IRRI).
  • Apply deep learning and ML to compare landrace and improved variety genomes.
  • Identify novel genetic variations linked to yield, stress tolerance, and nutritional quality.
  • Provide insights for germplasm utilization in rice and other crops.
  • Foster collaboration between genomics and computer science experts.

Genomic Data Analysis

Utilizing large datasets from CUK and IRRI repositories.

Machine Learning Integration

Applying cutting-edge ML/DL tools for genomic comparison and feature discovery.

Trait Discovery

Focusing on identifying genes for improved agricultural traits.
